About

I am a Senior Research Fellow at the Research Institute of Industrial Economics (IFN) and an Associate Professor in Economics. My research focuses on globalization, digitalization, international investment, and labor markets — with a particular interest in how technological change and multinational activity shape wages, mobility, and firm performance.

Much of my work uses rich Swedish matched employer–employee data and detailed firm microdata to study distributional consequences of globalization and technology, and to inform policy discussions.
