About

I am a Senior Research Fellow at the Research Institute of Industrial Economics (IFN) in Stockholm and an Associate Professor in Economics. My research focuses on globalization, digitalization, firm dynamics, and labor markets, with a particular interest in how technological change and shifting globalization patterns shape wages, mobility, and firm performance.
